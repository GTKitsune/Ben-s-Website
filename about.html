About The Artist

Benjamin Sequeira is South Florida based, Digital Illustrator, Machinamator, and Graphic Design. His Fine Art work deals with the ideals of blurring the line
between the real world and the virtual space, as well as raising the question in his work; whether videogames their communities mimic the real world or are they
truly part of it. "As we become a more technologically advance society, it becomes more intertwined with our everyday lives. Entire communities live sole through
the world wide web especially in video games such as MMO's. They do not mirror a society or mimic one, they are one."
Benjamin Digital Illustration also focus around telling stories especially through the environment. As well as working on Concept art for Enviornments, Characters,
and some prop work as part of his Digital Portfolio. Benjamin is a Highschool Graduate of The New World school of the arts, Visual arts Highschool program and is
currently attending their college program as Graphic Design Major.
